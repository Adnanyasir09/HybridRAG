Chicago, located in the state of Illinois, is the third-largest city in the United States. 
Known as "The Windy City," it is famous for its architectural innovation and skyline along Lake Michigan. 
Chicago has a strong cultural scene with theaters, museums, and music, especially blues and jazz. 
Famous landmarks include Willis Tower, Millennium Park, Navy Pier, and the Art Institute of Chicago. 
The city was founded in 1833 and became a major hub for railroads, industry, and finance.
