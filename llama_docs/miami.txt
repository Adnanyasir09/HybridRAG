Miami, located in Florida, is known for its beaches, nightlife, and cultural diversity. 
It is a major center for finance, commerce, culture, and international trade. 
Miami has a strong Latin American influence, visible in its cuisine, art, and music. 
Popular attractions include South Beach, Art Deco Historic District, Little Havana, and the Miami Seaquarium. 
The city was incorporated in 1896 and has a tropical monsoon climate.
