New York City (NYC), located in the state of New York, is the largest city in the United States by population. 
It consists of five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island. 
NYC is a global hub for finance, culture, media, and tourism. Landmarks include the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, 
Times Square, Empire State Building, and the Metropolitan Museum of Art. 
The city was originally settled by the Dutch in 1624 as New Amsterdam and renamed New York in 1664.
