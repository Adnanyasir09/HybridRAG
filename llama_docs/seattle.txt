Seattle is a major city in the state of Washington, known for its tech industry, coffee culture, and scenic surroundings. 
It is home to companies like Amazon and Microsoft and is famous for the Space Needle, Pike Place Market, and the Museum of Pop Culture. 
Seattle has a strong music scene and is the birthplace of grunge. 
The city is surrounded by water, mountains, and evergreen forests, giving it the nickname "Emerald City." 
Seattle was founded in 1851 and incorporated in 1869.
